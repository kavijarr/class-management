About Us

The Technology Section of Our School Had Been Started in Year 2013. There Were Two Classes As ET And BST. At That Time, We Didn’t Have Separate Classrooms and The Classes Were Conducted in Pavilion and Art Section Premises. We Got a New Technological Building In 2015 With 3 Floors. Two Extra Classes and Were Established Separately as Two BST Classes And Two ET Classes In 2019. By Completing Hard Journey, It Is Happy to Say We Have Reached the Most Perfect Section in Our School.
